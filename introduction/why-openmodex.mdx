## The Problem

Building with AI models today means dealing with fragmented infrastructure:

- **Multiple SDKs** — OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, and others each have their own client libraries, auth flows, and response formats
- **Vendor lock-in** — switching models means rewriting integration code
- **No reliability story** — when a provider goes down, your app goes down
- **Invisible costs** — separate billing across providers makes optimization impossible
- **No routing intelligence** — you hardcode which model to use, even when a cheaper or faster option exists

## Who Uses OpenModex

### Startups & Product Teams

Ship AI features fast without building provider abstraction layers. Start with one model, easily A/B test others, and scale without re-architecture.

### Platform & Infrastructure Teams

Centralize AI API access across your organization. Enforce rate limits, track costs per team, and maintain a single integration point.

### Agencies & Consultancies

Build client projects that aren't tied to a single provider. Recommend the best model for each use case and switch instantly when the landscape changes.
